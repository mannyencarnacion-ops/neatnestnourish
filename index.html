Good to Know

Questions, answered plainly

What products do you use?

Products are selected with your household in mind — chosen to be considerate of children, pets and sensitivities. If you would prefer we use your own supplies, we are glad to.

How long does a visit take?

Each service includes a labor window — two hours for a standard, pre-event or post-event clean, three hours for a deep or move-in/move-out clean. Additional time is $75 per hour and is always discussed with you first.

Do I need to be home?

Not at all. Many clients provide entry instructions and return to a finished home. Keys, codes and belongings are treated with complete discretion.

Is it the same cleaner every visit?

Yes. There are no rotating crews. The person who learns your home is the person who cares for it, visit after visit.

How is the final price decided?

The estimate tool gives an honest starting range. Final pricing is confirmed after a brief consultation, based on the size and condition of your home. Nothing is added afterward without your say.

Do you offer recurring cleaning?

Yes — bi-weekly visits save 10% and weekly visits save 15% off the standard rate. Recurring care can be paused or adjusted at any time.
